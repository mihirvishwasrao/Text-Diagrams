Theoretical models of Information Visualization

Text diagrams

Text diagrams provide a versatile and efficient way to convey information, offering simplicity, accessibility, and compatibility across various platforms and industries. Their importance lies in their ability to communicate complex concepts in a clear and universally accessible manner.

In this project, I have conducted an extensive literature review to pinpoint theoretical models concerning information visualization and delivery systems. These diagrams show the key concepts, frameworks, and explains the relationships that exist between them. It also uncovers the conceptual framework and draws inspiration from the theories that were uncovered in the literature. I have also assessed the efficacy of each model in terms of information delivery and its impact on user comprehension. Click Here to view all the articles.
